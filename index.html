About Me

Story

I graduated from Whitworth University in 2016 with a B.S. in engineering and took a job as a management consultant in Seattle, WA. After working for a few months, I knew I was in the wrong field and needed to make a change. I took some time to reflect on what I wanted out of a job and what career would best align my interests and lifestyle and stumbled upon web development. I was immediately hooked. I poured the majority of my free-time into coding (often neglecting friends) to build out projects and improve my skills in the hopes of transitioning into something I loved doing.

What I'm Looking For

Mostly, a company I can get behind. To me, a company is more than just a product; It's a culture, a way of doing things, and a group of people striving towards a similar goal. There should be more to work than just a paycheck and more to life than just work. Ideally, I want to be a part of something employees feel inspired about creating and delivering.
