About Me

Story

After graduating from Whitworth University in 2016 with a B.S. in engineering I took a job as a management consultant in Seattle, WA. After a short few months on the job I knew I was in the wrong field. After taking some time to reflect on what I wanted out of a job I stumbled across web development and was hooked. I poured the majority of my free-time into coding (often neglecting friends) and knew I had found a career. Fast forward to today and I think I'm ready to make the jump.

What I'm Looking For

Mostly, a company I can get behind. I want to work somewhere I feel passionate enought about to go above and beyond for. To me, a company is more than just a product; It's a culture, a way of doing things, and group of similarly passioned individuals. I want to work with poeple who aren't afraid of breaking the status quo or taking a chance on something new.
